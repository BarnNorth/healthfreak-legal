Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use my free scans?

A: Just take a photo of any ingredient list! Your first 10 scans include full analysis with detailed wellness insights.

Q: What happens after 10 scans?

A: You'll still have access to your scan history. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited scans at $10/month.

Q: How do I upgrade to Premium?

A: Tap "Upgrade to Premium" in the Profile tab. Choose Apple In-App Purchase or Credit Card payment.

Q: How accurate is the AI analysis?

A: Our AI is trained on functional medicine principles and holistic health research. Results are educational and help you make informed choices. This is not medical advice.

Q: Can I delete my account?

A: Yes! Email us at support@healthfreak.io and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours.

Q: The app won't scan my label - help?

A: Make sure the ingredient list is well-lit and in focus. Hold your phone steady for 2-3 seconds. If issues persist, try taking the photo from a different angle or with better lighting.

Q: How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

A: For Apple subscriptions: Go to iPhone Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → Health Freak → Cancel. For Stripe subscriptions: Tap "Manage Subscription" in the app's Profile tab.
